04

Bookings and Cancellation Policy

Making a Booking

When a Player selects a time slot and completes payment, the booking is confirmed. Both parties receive an SMS confirmation. The Coach's phone number is shared with the Player after payment to allow session coordination (court location, access, etc.).

Slots are held for 10 minutes during checkout. If payment is not completed within that window, the slot is released.

Cancellation Policy

Player cancels more than 24 hours before the session: Full refund. No fee.

Player cancels less than 24 hours before the session: 50% refund. The remaining 50% is split — 30% to the Coach (for reserved time), 20% to LOBB.

Coach cancels at any time: Player receives a full refund. Coach receives one strike on their account.

Coach no-show: Player receives a full refund. Coach receives one strike.

Player no-show: No refund. Coach receives 50% of the session fee via automatic payout release.

Three strikes on a Coach account triggers an account review and potential suspension.

Court Access

LOBB does not provide or guarantee court access. Each Coach profile states whether the coach has court access or whether the Player must arrange it. Players should confirm court logistics directly with the Coach after booking is confirmed.

05

Payment Terms

How Payment Works

All payments on LOBB are processed securely through Paystack, a licensed payment service provider in Nigeria. LOBB does not store your card details.

The total amount charged to the Player is the Coach's listed session rate plus a 5% LOBB service fee.

Example: A ₦20,000 session = ₦20,000 (Coach rate) + ₦1,000 (5% LOBB fee) = ₦21,000 total.

How Coach Payouts Work

LOBB holds the payment after a Player pays. The Coach does not receive funds immediately. Two hours after a session's scheduled start time, LOBB releases 85% of the Coach's session rate to the Coach's registered bank account. LOBB retains 15% of the session rate as its commission.

This means if a session does not happen as expected, LOBB can manage refunds before releasing funds. It is not an escrow service — LOBB itself is the party holding funds temporarily before disbursing them.

Refund Timing

Refunds are processed through Paystack and typically appear within 3–7 business days depending on your bank. LOBB does not control the speed of bank processing.

Disputes Over Payment

If you believe a payment error has occurred, contact support@lobb.ng within 48 hours of the session date. LOBB will investigate and respond within 5 business days.
