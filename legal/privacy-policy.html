Data We Collect Automatically

  • Device and browser information — type of device, browser version, operating system. Used to ensure the app works correctly on your device.
  • Usage data — which pages you visit, which coaches you view, how long you spend on pages. Used to improve the platform.
  • IP address — used for security (detecting unusual login attempts) and approximate location.
  • Session data — booking history, cancellation history, review history.

What We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect card numbers or payment credentials. These are handled entirely by Paystack.
  • We do not collect biometric data (fingerprints, facial recognition) directly. If an identity verification provider uses biometrics, their own privacy policy applies to that step.

Third-Party Services We Use

LOBB shares limited data with the following providers to operate the platform. Each provider is bound by their own privacy policies and, where applicable, data processing agreements with LOBB.

Paystack
Payment processing — handles all card and bank transfer transactions

Data shared: name, email, phone number, bank account details (for coaches). Paystack is a licensed Payment Service Provider under the Central Bank of Nigeria. Privacy policy: paystack.com/privacy

Termii
SMS delivery — sends OTP codes, booking confirmations, and reminders

Data shared: phone number and SMS message content only.

Smile ID / Identity Verification Provider
Coach identity verification — verifies NIN and BVN against government records

Data shared: coach's NIN, BVN, full name, date of birth. This provider processes sensitive identity data under a separate data processing agreement with LOBB. You give explicit, separate consent for this step during coach onboarding.

Supabase
Database and file storage — stores all platform data securely

Infrastructure provider. Data remains within LOBB's controlled environment.

Posthog
Product analytics — helps us understand how the platform is used

Data shared: anonymised usage events, page views, and interaction data. No personally identifiable information is included in analytics events.

06

Cookies and Tracking

LOBB uses a small number of cookies and similar technologies:

  • Session cookie — keeps you logged in while using the app. Strictly necessary. Cannot be disabled.
  • Referral tracking cookie — records which coach's referral link you arrived through, for 30 days, to attribute referral commissions correctly. Set only when you click a referral link.
  • Analytics cookie (Posthog) — tracks anonymised page views and interactions to improve the platform. You can opt out by adjusting your browser settings.

LOBB does not use advertising cookies or sell data to advertising networks.

07

Your Rights Under Nigerian Data Protection Law

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act (NDPA) 2023, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data LOBB holds about you
  • Correction — ask us to correct any inaccurate data
  • Deletion — ask us to delete your data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Restriction — ask us to stop processing your data in certain circumstances
  • Portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interest, including direct marketing
  • Withdraw consent — where we rely on consent, you can withdraw it at any time

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@lobb.ng. We will respond within 30 days. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may contact the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) at ndpc.gov.ng.

08

Data Security

LOBB takes reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted (HTTPS/TLS).
  • Sensitive data (NIN, BVN, bank details) is stored encrypted at rest.
  • Access to user data within LOBB is restricted to staff who genuinely need it.
  • Paystack handles all card data — we never see or store raw card numbers.

No system is 100% secure. If a data breach occurs that is likely to affect your rights, we will notify you and the NDPC within 72 hours of discovering it, as required by law.
