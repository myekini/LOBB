## Users
- **Players**: Tennis players in Lagos, Nigeria looking to discover verified coaches, book hourly sessions, pick courts, and pay securely.
- **Coaches**: Professional tennis instructors in Lagos who manage their availability, receive session bookings, track their earnings, and receive bank payouts.
- **Admins**: LOBB operators who audit coach applications, monitor bookings/earnings, and manage payout triggers.

## Product Purpose
LOBB is a premium Lagos-based tennis coaching marketplace. It replaces manual scheduling with verified real-time calendars, a 10-minute checkout slot lock, and secure split-escrow payments via Paystack.

## Brand Personality
- **Active / Athletic**: Bold and structured, referencing the energy of the sport.
- **Local-Authentic**: Proudly Lagosian, tailored to local landmarks like the National Stadium.
- **Operational / Premium**: Precise, clean, and highly trustworthy, avoiding corporate jargon or generic SaaS templates.

## Anti-references
- **SaaS Monoculture**: Avoid generic creamy/beige templates, soft gray text on near-white, or generic bento grids.
- **Overly Rounded Aesthetics**: No large corner radii (max card radius 12–16px; inputs/dropdowns are crisp).
- **Decorative Slop**: No text gradients, decorative glassmorphism, or generic sketchy SVGs/stripes.

## Design Principles
- **"Saturday Morning Court"**: Uses a high-contrast layout anchored by clay orange (`#C4622D`), deep charcoal (`#1A1714`), and warm court paper (`#FAF8F5`).
- **Flow Integrity**: Zero distraction during scheduling and checkout. Every interface element serves the transactional goal.
- **App-Canvas Architecture**: Smooth, responsive layouts that feel native on mobile (via bottom tabs) and clean on desktop (via top header navigations).

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios >= 4.5:1 for body and placeholder text to guarantee outdoor readability.
- Clear tactile feedback and interactive hit targets >= 44px.
- Full support for `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` to disable transitions and animations.
